Flood Flag

SHAD Carleton DE Project

After a flood, rescuers can't see you.

During floods, people are often trapped in the top floor or the roof of their homes, and can't contact emergency services. Flood Flag allows homeowners to communicate with emergency services and increase their chances of being rescued. When the power goes out, the Flood Flag's reflective material, LED lights, and customizable message symbols provide a much better alternative to traditional means of signalling like drawing on the roof or using mirrors.

Main Features

interchangeable Pictograms

Attach these reflective canvas symbols onto the flag with snap on buttons to communicate to emergency services.

WILL YOU BE PREPARED?

How it Works

Install on your wall

Attach our wall clip system outside your house ahead of time, so that you'll be prepared if there is a flood.

Display outside your window

Our flag can fit any size of window. Simply hook it onto the pre-installed wall rings with carabiner clips.

Customize your message

Our interchangeable symbols ensure that you can communicate your needs for food, water, or medical supplies to emergency services.

About Us

We are a group of students from across Canada that have been identified as high potential due to our success in STEM related fields and our involvement throughout the community. While some of our team members have experienced flooding first hand, all of our group members have extensively researched the challenges homeowners and rescue services face during flooding. We have called various organizations and contacted experts in this field to settle on a product that will save time, money and lives during flooding. Our team is passionate about resilience during natural disasters and creating a simple but effective solution to aid community recovery efforts.
