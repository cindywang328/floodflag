Flood Flag

SHAD Carleton DE Project

Find people in an emergency

During floods, people are often trapped in the top floor or the roof of their homes, and can't contact emergency services. Flood Flag allows homeowners to communicate with emergency services and increase their chances of being rescued.

About Us

Our team is passionate about resilience during natural disasters and creating a simple but effective solution to aid community recovery efforts.

How it Works

Clamp to the window

Our retractable pole system allows you to clamp the Flood Flag to a window or other structures in your house.

Attach outside your house

Our versatile design enables the Flood Flag to be attached anywhere on your house, depending on the situation.

Signal for help

Our interchangeable signals ensure that you can communicate your needs for food, water, or medical supplies to emergency services.
